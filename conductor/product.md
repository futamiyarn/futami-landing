# Initial Concept
A Futami "Linktree" style landing page built with Astro and served on Cloudflare Pages.

# Product Definition

## Vision
To provide a centralized hub for Futami's audience to access all content platforms and products. This platform serves as a showcase for various channel links and brand products, ensuring fans can easily navigate the Futami ecosystem.

## Target Audience
The primary audience consists of fans, followers, and supporters who want a convenient way to stay updated with Futami's latest activities and find ways to provide financial support through donations or affiliates.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Centralized Content**: A single destination for YouTube videos, blog posts, and social media links.
- **Enhanced Discoverability**: Streamlined navigation to both content and support channels.
- **High Performance**: Optimized using Astro and Cloudflare KV for fast load times and reliable data fetching.

## Key Features
- **YouTube Integration**: Real-time display of the latest channel videos with performance caching.
- **Blog Feed Integration**: Dynamic aggregation of recent articles from the Futami blog API.
- **Support & Affiliate Portal**: Dedicated sections for donations and affiliate product links to facilitate community support.

## Strategic Goals
- **Content Aggregation**: Consolidate diverse content streams into a cohesive user experience.
- **Platform Growth**: Drive traffic to secondary platforms (Blog, TikTok, etc.) from the main hub.
- **Sustainability**: Optimize the support flow to ensure long-term sustainability of the Futami brand.
